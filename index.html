Arnold Schwarzenegger

Bodybuilder | Actor | Governor

Timeline

  • 1947: Born in Thal, Austria.
  • 1967: Earned 'Mr Universe' title
  • 1970: Won the Mr Olympia title
  • 1970's: Stepped into Hollywood and began his career with the film ‘Hercules in New York’
  • 1971 - 75: Winner of Mr. Olympia contest and in 1975, he declared his intention to withdraw from professional body-building
  • 1976: his film ‘Stay Hungry’ was released in which his acting got appreciated and he also won an award for this film.
  • 1980: Won Mr. Olympia competition and officially took retirement from professional body-building.
  • 1982: he got his first major hit when his film ‘Conan the Barbarian’ was released. He then worked on the sequel of this film called ‘Conan the Destroyer’. This was followed by many more films such as ‘The Terminator’, ‘Predator’, ‘Commando’, ‘The Running Man’, ‘Raw Deal’, Red Heat’ which launched him as a successful action hero.
  • 1988: He did a comic role in the film ‘Twins’. This was followed by his film ‘Total Recall’ which was a huge commercial success.
  • 1990 - 93: he chaired the ‘President's Council on Physical Fitness and Sports’ and was then the chairperson of the ‘California Governor's Council on Physical Fitness and Sports’. He was also the ambassador of the ‘Red Cross’ which is an international humanitarian movement.
  • 1992: he played the protagonist in the ‘Terminator 2: Judgement Day’ which was immensely successful and became the biggest commercial hit of that year.
  • 1994: He worked in the film ‘True Lies’, and the film ‘Junior’ which earned him a ‘Golden Globe Award’ nomination
  • 2003: Was elected Governor of California.
  • 2011: Completed his second term as the Governor of California, he decided to make a comeback in Hollywood.
  • 2012: His autobiography named ‘Total Recall: My Unbelievably True Life Story’ was published.
